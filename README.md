# HoTsauce
/*
ingredients:
9 Bramley Apples
2 x 568ml Malt Vinegar
12oz Dark brown sugar
4oz White sugar
8 oz Sultanas
2 Lemons – juice and zest
4 Onions
2 Garlic bulbs, not skinned – but bashed
3 heaped Tbsp Turmeric
¼ Cup Anchovy Sauce
¼ Cup Soy Sauce
3 Tbsp Gochugaro Paste (Korean Hot Pepper Paste)
1-2 oz Dried Birds Eye Chillies
2 Tbsp Mixed Spice or Cinnamon Bark
5 Star Anise
1 Tbsp Cloves } grind together in pestil &amp; mortar
2 Tbsp Green Cardamom } then remove skins
2 Tbsp Black Pepper
1 Tbsp Salt
2 oz Fresh Ginger

Method:
Peel and chop apples. Combine apples, vinegar, sugars, sultanas and lemon and
boil for approximately 20 minutes.
Blitz together remaining ingredients and add to apples. Cook for 30 – 40
minutes then blitz with hand blitzer.
If it’s a bit too hot, you can add some apple puree.
*/
